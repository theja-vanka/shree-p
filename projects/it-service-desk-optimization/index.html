Overview

An 8-week engagement (Q3 2024) to reduce resolution time and improve customer satisfaction across incident, problem, and change management using ITIL frameworks.

Approach

  1. 1Incident management: fixed bottlenecks in ticket logging/categorization and added automated ticket routing
  2. 2Problem management: shifted from reactive to proactive, with enhanced root-cause analysis
  3. 3Change management: streamlined approvals and introduced post-implementation reviews

Key Findings

  • Average resolution time was 6 hours with a backlog of 350+ tickets
  • First call resolution was only 65%
  • Inefficiencies cost an estimated €1.2M annually

Recommendations

  • Build a comprehensive knowledge base
  • Introduce proactive problem management
